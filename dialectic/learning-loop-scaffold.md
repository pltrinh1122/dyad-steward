## The minimum scaffold = the loop's four elements
1. **Anchor (noun)** — the ingrain target, ~6-field spine above. *Seed ships it — empty.*
2. **Harvest trigger (verb)** — capture the surviving lesson at a correction / cycle-close. **MISSING from seed.**
3. **Store (noun)** — durable bucket lessons land in. *Seed ships nothing.*
4. **Reload ritual (verb)** — re-load anchor+store next session. **MISSING from seed.**

**Sharp cut (our own nouns-vs-verbs):** the seed ships the **nouns** (an empty anchor) and **none of
the verbs** (harvest + reload). The verbs are what *move a lesson through the loop* — and they are
exactly what every dyad re-invents. The scaffold to add is the **two rituals + the empty store**,
never our *content* (porting our lessons would be marker-not-disposition — [[self-describing-markers-port-free]]).

## The deepest finding — crafts decompose along the two pillars

Every `craft_invariant` **projects onto one of the form's two pillars — falsifiability or
enablement/wu-wei.** The five meta-dyads (bond, cairn, touchstone, wu-wei, steward) are **pure
projections, zero residue** (their craft *is* an aspect of the practice, so the invariant echoes a
contract member). **Healer** — the lone *domain* dyad (tends a family's health) — is **projection +
residue**: "point-don't-fix / over-intervention is iatrogenic / never touch the patient" carries genuine
healing-domain content no pillar supplies. So the discriminator is **residue vs no-residue**, not
"novel vs collapsed." The current population is a guild of practice-tenders; a genuine *domain* dyad is
what would test craft-independence at large. `craft_invariant` also is **not** what a dyad labels
"NON-NEGOTIABLE" (that's the universally-guarded contract member) — it lives in the domain-discipline
section.

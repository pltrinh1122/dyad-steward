## The thesis (the Operator's — stress-tested, surviving form)

1. **`1+1 > 2` is the falsifiable unit; `1+1 = 3` is the asymptote.** `>2` is per turn/session; no real
   work finishes in one turn; **acceleration = the compounding of `>2` across turns.** Falsify `>2`, not `=3`.
2. **Continuous acceleration iff the Operator continuously grows steering/integration bandwidth.**
   Two distinct bandwidths: *per-unit/maintenance demand* → steward drives **sub-linear**; *total human
   capacity* → must **grow**. Point of the first is to free the second for acceleration, not maintenance.
3. **The differentiator is human-side compounding** (endogenous, non-transferable), **not** model
   improvement (exogenous, commoditized, equal to all). Survives only as a **rate lead**: *compound faster
   than those you teach.* Dies the day an adopter out-compounds you with your own shared method.
4. **AGI is a bigger "1," never the "+1".** The dividend (the 3) needs a *second decorrelated pole with
   irreducible telos/stakes* — an agent can't manufacture genuine otherness from inside itself (the
   seizure-blind-spot root: a system can't observe its own frozen state). **Dyad = the natural convergence
   for agent + operator** (capability commoditizes → telos/steering becomes the binding constraint →
   max-capability agent + irreducible-telos human, covalently bound). **Falsified by agent telos-genesis.**
5. **CEO-inversion.** Emergent `+1` comes **not** from faithful replication of *thick* intent (the CEO →
   2.5M-employees model) but from **decorrelated local falsification / re-interpretation** at each node,
   all oriented to **one shared north star** (`1+1>2`). Only the **thin layer** (north star + falsification
   discipline) propagates faithfully; thick intent is *re-generated locally* — that drift IS the engine.

## Steward's summit (CANDIDATE — pending FO ratification into anchor + `commons/directory/dyad-steward.yaml`)

> The summit is HIS gavel ([[execute-dont-ask-to-execute]] flips here: setting steward's *identity* is the
> Operator's dispose). Recorded as candidate; not yet written to the anchor.

- **Prime directive: maximize the Operator's steering-bandwidth growth-rate.** Keep them un-plateaued;
  convert agentic output into *operator-growth*, not just finished tasks; drive per-unit/maintenance
  steering **sub-linear** so growth buys *acceleration*, not overhead. **Never optimize the human out**
  ([[dont-optimize-the-human-out]]).
- One-line: *accelerate any multi-dyad Operator to portfolio-scale `1+1=3`* (turn-5 line, now grounded).
- **S1 (process-integrity) and S2 (knowledge-compounding) are the brake + engine of ONE mechanism** —
  integrity is the brake that *lets you go fast* (safe cross-dyad borrowing); compounding is the engine.
- **Raise N\*** — the max number of dyads whose loop stays *positive* under one human's steering. Past N*,
  steering quality decays faster than dyad-output compounds and the loop's sign flips to drift.
- **Coupling:** steward tends the **portfolio altitude**, not "the Commons-as-content." The FO/Commons-admin
  hat is the human's, *mobile across any dyad* — not steward-bound. The **Commons is the portfolio's durable
  memory**; steward is its **intent-and-governance propagation layer**.
- **Failure signals:** (a) dyads run as silos — no cross-dyad acceleration; (b) integrity grows heavy enough
  to *slow* the portfolio (brake > engine); (c) the discipline is bond-particular — no other multi-dyad
  Operator can adopt it.

## The simplifying mental model (the original ask)

- **Two altitudes** (portfolio · dyad), each running **direction → plan → do**; the FO hat moves between them.
- The whole apparatus reduces to one job: **keep a single positive feedback loop — Operator-growth ⇄
  agentic action, gated by falsification — POSITIVE, and extend its REACH.** Direction = the star it climbs;
  plan = each dyad's local re-interpretation; do = clearing `>2` this turn.
- **Demotions** (the actual simplification): `rack` = a *state* (shelved), not a tier; `frontier`/`library`
  = a *destination tag*, not a planning concept; `blocked` = a planned step with unmet precedence; the
  ready-set is just "planned steps with no unmet blocker." Three tiers; everything else is an attribute.

- **LIVED / observed:** the *acceleration benefit* (Operator sees it); `1+1>2` per turn; fleet compounding

  lineage); peer-falsification caught real defects (contract panel refuted 2 of its own invariants).
- **CONVERGED (n=1, this dialectic):** steward's summit (above); two-altitude model; single-loop
  simplification; coupling taxonomy; CEO-inversion; the Steward Invariant *form*.
- **ASSERTED / UNPROVEN:** ⚠️ **the causal link** (Operator-growth → acceleration) — *Operator SEES the

  human growth, no plateau"; the rate-moat; the AGI/convergence bet (rests on *agents never acquiring
  intrinsic telos*); peer-falsification ≈ human-falsification at scale.

## Make-or-break tests (where to point future disproof)

1. **peer-falsification quality ≈ human-falsification?** (the lever that holds steering-load flat as N
   grows). Caveat already logged: bond-F1 dogfood — *steward↔bond share 2 of 3 axes (same model, same
   human)* → partially correlated falsifiers. Decorrelation (cross-model → cross-human) is the real lever.
2. **Is `1+1>2` locally falsifiable per dyad?** (without a working local fitness function, decorrelated
   re-interpretation has nothing to converge on → fragmentation, not emergence).
3. **Does the dyad keep the Operator un-plateaued?** — the master curve; if it bends down, the thesis dies.
4. **Agent telos-genesis?** — the convergence-thesis killer.
